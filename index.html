About Me

Since 1973, we are in premium provider of Professional photography.
Sangam Studio, has established itself as a trusted expert for all kinds of photographic needs by making the process of being photographed a joyful experience and consistently encapsulating moments into beautiful snapshots of memories.

In these four decades while we have been working with commitment, dedication and trust, the unwavering faith and confidence of our clients has helped us grow tremendously.

Our wedding photography business is here to provide you with expert assistance. Whatever your needs may be, get in touch with us today.
