Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the app without any payment?

Yes. Just download the app, register and start using.

Which businesses benefit from Pepo?

Any business which uses Salesmen to collect orders from its clients. For example, FMCG companies, Pharma distributors or any SME

What is a Buyer and Seller?

Seller is anyone who wishes to sell/distribute products. Sellers can build their own product catalogue on the app, list down phone nos of its customers and start taking orders from them. Buyer is any business (retailer, medical shop, restaurant, etc) that orders products from many suppliers. Any user of the app can switch between Buyer and Seller modes.

My brand reaches 10,000 retailers through 50 distributors. Can I use the app?

Yes. We will help you in setting up a centralised ordering system, separate product cataloguing for each distributor, distributor-retailer mapping, etc. Just fill your details on the Contact Us page and we will guide you.
